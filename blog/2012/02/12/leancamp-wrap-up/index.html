Leancamp Wrap-up

Feb 12th, 2012

Update: Leancamp is coming to Madrid next week, October 27th. It’s an awesome opportunity to meet others in the startup community and to learn a lot. Don’t miss it!

One of my goals in attending Leancamp Barcelona was to meet people in the software development and startup communities in Barcelona and around the world. To prepare for that, I compiled a list of blogs, startups, and Twitter accounts that I found interesting from the list of attendees posted before the event, where everyone posted their blog, website, or Twitter account. I also included people, startups, and organizations I met at Leancamp, as well as recommendations from a friend.

Enjoy! If I missed anyone just shoot me message at @alourenco Follow or abraao@alourenco.com.

Events and meetups

Barcelona

  • ESADE CodeCamp: every 1st and 3rd Thursday of the month. FREE, but you have to register ahead of time. Headed by Jan Brinckmann (@janbri Follow )
  • Agile Barcelona(@agilebcn Follow ) organizes meetups, talks, and coding dojos.
  • Check La Salle Technova Barcelona Blog on startups for new events.

Spain

  • BetaBeers(@betabeers Follow ): monthly meeting of web and app developers around Spain (including Barcelona).
  • Union of Student Entrepreneurs(@e3union Follow )

Europe

  • AltLab, Lisbon’s Hackerspace(@altlab Follow )
